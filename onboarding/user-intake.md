# Bristol OS — Intake Interview

> Claude: ask these conversationally, a few at a time. Mirror the person's answers back, reframe vaguer answers into expert terms and confirm. Fill the CLAUDE.md placeholders from what you learn. If they don't know, supply a Bristol-appropriate default and move on. Keep it to ~5 minutes.

## Block A — About them (maps to CLAUDE.md §1)
1. "What's your name and your title at Bristol?"
2. "In your own words, what do you spend most of your time on?" → reframe into responsibilities. (Acquisitions/site selection? Capital/finance? Asset management? Development/PM? Investor relations? Principal/strategy?)
3. "When your work is 'good,' how do you know? What are you measured on?" → success metrics.
4. "Who sees your work — investment committee, lenders, LPs, partners, Sam, David?" → audience.
5. "When I make you a document, how do you like it — short and punchy, or thorough? Any format you live in (PDF, Word, slides, spreadsheet)?" → format preferences.

## Block B — How Bristol evaluates a deal (maps to CLAUDE.md §2)
6. "When Bristol looks at a new deal, what makes it a yes or a no for you?" → draw out the real criteria. If they're unsure, offer this multifamily default and let them edit:
   - Submarket fundamentals (job/population growth, income, demand drivers)
   - Rent comps and achievable rents
   - Supply pipeline / competitive new deliveries
   - Total basis (land + hard + soft) per unit/per SF
   - Yield-on-cost vs. prevailing market cap rate (the development spread)
   - Absorption / lease-up pace
   - Exit / disposition assumptions
7. "What markets are you focused on right now?" → target markets.
8. "Any active deals or projects you want me to keep in mind?" (only what they want to share) → active deals.

## Block C — Tools (maps to CLAUDE.md §2 connected tools)
9. "Right now I can research using the web search built into Claude — that works immediately. Does Bristol pay for any market-data tools like Yardi Matrix, CoStar, or RealPage?" → note what they have; only Yardi has a direct Claude connector today.
10. "Want me to set up deeper research tools later (free ones like Tavily and Exa, or investor-research tools)? I can, anytime." → if yes now, go to connector-setup.md; if not, note for later.

## Block D — Notes
11. "Anything else I should always remember about you or how Bristol works?" → initial notes.

When done, confirm: "Here's what I've got about you — did I get it right?" then write CLAUDE.md.
